Jahongir Travel Homepage – UI/UX Design Audit

------------------------------------------------------
GENERAL OVERVIEW
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Professional and visually cohesive layout.
- Strong brand palette (navy, gold, white).
- Excellent section hierarchy and spacing rhythm.
- Consistent typography and CTA clarity.

Weaknesses:
- Visual density in mid sections (similar card size).
- Minor contrast and whitespace inconsistencies.
- Hero section hierarchy and alignment could improve.
- Accessibility and hover feedback can be refined.

------------------------------------------------------
1. HERO SECTION ("Discover the Magic of Uzbekistan")
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Strong hero image (Registan) with cultural impact.
- Serif + sans-serif combo communicates trust and elegance.
- CTA visible above the fold.

Improvements:
- Add darker overlay gradient for better contrast (target ≥4.5:1).
- Tighten vertical spacing (reduce 8–12px between subtitle & CTA).
- Use gold primary CTA and white outline secondary for clarity.
- Adjust vertical position for mobile hero text (upper third).

------------------------------------------------------
2. WHY WE’RE YOUR PERFECT TRAVEL PARTNER
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Great trust-building section with ratings and contact icons.
- Warm visuals through image gallery.

Improvements:
- Increase top/bottom padding.
- Group contact methods visually in subtle surface card.
- Increase image radius to match global token (12px).
- Improve paragraph spacing (line-height 1.6).

------------------------------------------------------
3. TRENDING ACTIVITIES IN UZBEKISTAN
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Clear segmentation by interest categories.
- Consistent card icons and titles.

Improvements:
- Ensure hover state brightens slightly (saturate +5%).
- Maintain 24px vertical padding inside cards.
- Add short intro sentence above heading for context.

------------------------------------------------------
4. EXPLORE POPULAR UZBEKISTAN TOURS
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Tour card layout is clear and readable.
- CTAs are consistent and visually appealing.

Improvements:
- Align all tour image heights.
- Make price bolder or highlighted with color.
- Standardize tag color (“NEW”, “POPULAR”). 
- Add section divider or tone variation for rhythm.

------------------------------------------------------
5. TOP PLACES TO TRAVEL
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Elegant grayscale overlay cards with simple layout.

Improvements:
- Reduce overlay opacity for image clarity.
- Add hover zoom/lighten effect for interactivity.
- Move “Browse” button to hover state to reduce clutter.

------------------------------------------------------
6. TRAVELLER REVIEWS
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Well-aligned, scannable testimonial layout.

Improvements:
- Add subtle card shadow for contrast against white bg.
- Emphasize rating and TripAdvisor source visually.
- Consider carousel for mobile view.

------------------------------------------------------
7. TRAVEL INSIGHTS & TIPS
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Vibrant, modern pastel cards.
- Nice typography contrast for blog content.

Improvements:
- Standardize image corners and alignment.
- Place category chip above title (smaller, uppercase).
- Add 64px top margin for section spacing.

------------------------------------------------------
8. FOOTER
------------------------------------------------------
Strengths:
- Logical structure: logo → navigation → contact → socials.
- Strong dark contrast with clear legibility.

Improvements:
- Increase top padding (80px) and column spacing.
- Slightly enlarge font size (15–16px).
- Add subtle gold underline under headings for branding.

------------------------------------------------------
VISUAL CONSISTENCY
------------------------------------------------------
- Color: navy + gold + white strong; introduce neutral gray (#F8F9FB) sections.
- Typography: maintain modular scale, avoid large jumps.
- Buttons: add hover elevation (box-shadow var(--shadow-2)).
- Radius: unify all cards/buttons to 12px.
- Shadows: keep opacity consistent (8–12%).

------------------------------------------------------
ACCESSIBILITY & PERFORMANCE
------------------------------------------------------
- Ensure all text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios.
- Add aria-labels for icons and links.
- Verify keyboard focus visibility.
- Optimize images (WebP/AVIF + lazy-load).

------------------------------------------------------
UX ENHANCEMENT IDEAS
------------------------------------------------------
1. Add sticky CTA header (“Plan My Trip”). 
2. Alternate white/gray background per section for rhythm.
3. Subtle fade-in on scroll (200–300ms).
4. Personalized “Recommended Tours” module.
5. Refine CTA copy to more descriptive text.
