AI Coder Brief — “Personality” Section for Contact Page
===========================================================
Project: Jahongir Travel — Contact Page
Block Name: Faces Behind Every Journey (Personality Section)
Version: 1.0
Authoring Date: 2025‑10‑30

Goal
----
Add a warm, human “Personality” section directly below the Contact form block and above the FAQ. The section shows a real team/office photo with short copy that reinforces the brand’s human, local identity — cohesive in layout, colors, spacing, and typography with the current page.

Success Criteria (Acceptance Tests)
-----------------------------------
1) The section renders directly below the Contact block and above FAQ on desktop and mobile.
2) The section matches existing page spacing rhythm (vertical rhythm identical to adjacent sections).
3) The image uses rounded corners and soft shadow, and loads as WebP with responsive sizes.
4) The copy matches the text provided below and line-lengths are readable (55–75ch desktop).
5) The section passes a11y checks (alt text, heading levels, contrast, keyboard focus on CTA).
6) Lighthouse Performance ≥ 90 and Accessibility ≥ 90 (after image optimization).
7) No reflow/jumps when fonts or image load (use width/height or aspect-ratio).

Placement
---------
- Insert after: Contact block (form + sidebar).
- Insert before: FAQ block.
- Section ID: contact-personality
- HTML landmark: <section id="contact-personality" aria-labelledby="contact-personality-title">

  Faces behind every journey

Accessibility
-------------
- Heading order: H1 (page) → H2 (this section). Do not skip levels.
- Alt text: meaningful; no “image of…”. Keep: “Jahongir Travel team in Samarkand office”.
- Link name clarity: “Meet the Team” makes sense out of context.
- Focus states: inherit site’s button/input focus outline. Confirm visible on keyboard tab.
- Color contrast: ensure text on #FAFAF8 meets WCAG AA (≥ 4.5:1 for body copy). If needed, darken to #4B5563.

Performance
-----------
- Serve WebP with srcset & sizes (provided). Keep width/height attributes to avoid CLS.
- Preload the hero fonts only; do NOT preload this section’s image.
- Largest contentful element on this section should not exceed 350 KB.
- Set loading=“lazy” and decoding=“async” on the image.

Analytics (optional)
